Privacy Policy

Draft — not published. Effective date will be set when this policy is hosted on HTTPS.

This privacy policy describes how Shopping Kart / Smart Kart (the “App”) handles information in its current closed-test build. The App is a basket comparison utility. It does not process payments, execute checkout, or operate as a live commerce platform in this build.

1. Who we are

Developer contact: shahvishal007@gmail.com. For support options, see the Support / Contact page.

2. Data we collect

The closed-test App may handle the following categories of data:

Data type Collected? Shared? Notes
Account identifiers Synthetic test only No Used for closed-test sign-in and account-linked features. Deletion supported.
Search history Yes (local / synthetic) No Recent searches stored for convenience; purged on account deletion.
Location (pincode) Yes (local entry only) No User-entered pincode for availability checks. No GPS or background location.
Financial data No No No checkout, payment, or order execution in closed-test build.
Device or advertising IDs No No No analytics SDK or advertising ID collection.

3. How we use data

  • Authenticate and maintain synthetic closed-test accounts.
  • Remember recent searches and pincode preferences on the device or test backend.
  • Compare basket options and show savings explanations in the closed-test experience.
  • Respond to account and data deletion requests.

We do not sell personal data. We do not show ads in the closed-test reference build.

4. Data retention and deletion

Account-linked data categories that can be deleted include: profile, consent preferences, saved addresses, recent searches, sessions, and linked customer records. Users may request deletion via the Account & Data Deletion page once hosting is live.

Local-only data may also be cleared by uninstalling the App or resetting app data on the device.

5. Security

The closed-test build is designed without unnecessary network permissions in the Android manifest. When backend services are used in test environments, transport security requirements apply per environment policy. Production release remains blocked pending founder approval and release gates.

6. Children

The App is not directed to children. It is intended for a general audience (18+).

7. Changes to this policy

This draft will be updated before any public store listing. Material changes will be reflected on this page with a revised effective date.
