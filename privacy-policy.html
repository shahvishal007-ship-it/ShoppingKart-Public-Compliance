Privacy Policy

This policy explains data handling for the current TOLZA closed-test Android build and this public information website.

Effective date17 July 2026
Applies toTOLZA closed-test app and public website
Contactshahvishal007@gmail.com
Closed-test scope: the current submitted Android build uses synthetic demonstration data, has no Internet permission and does not transmit Google Play “required user data types” off the device.

1. Who we are

TOLZA (Android package com.shoppingkart.app) is a shopping-decision product under development. The current release is an invitation-only closed test intended to validate user experience, multilingual search, basket management and synthetic comparison flows.

2. Data handling in the current Android Alpha

The current Alpha processes synthetic test inputs locally on the device. It does not connect to live retailer services, does not show live prices or stock, and does not send required user data types off the device.

Test inputs may include a synthetic name, test phone number, fixed test OTP, pincode, search terms, basket contents, language selection and locally displayed settings. These inputs are used to operate the test journey and are not transmitted to a TOLZA production backend in this build.

3. Location

The current submitted build does not provide production GPS integration. It does not share a user’s precise physical location with other users. Any future location feature will require appropriate permission, a clear purpose and an updated privacy disclosure before release.

4. This public website

This website is a static information and compliance site. It does not use account registration, advertising cookies, analytics trackers, behavioural profiling or a database. Standard infrastructure logs may be processed by the hosting provider for security and service operation.

5. Support communications

If you email support, we receive the information you choose to include, such as your email address, device details and description of an issue. We use it only to respond, investigate the request and maintain necessary support records. Do not send passwords, one-time passwords, payment information or unnecessary sensitive information.

6. Sharing and sale of data

We do not sell personal data. The current closed-test build does not share required user data types with advertisers or data brokers. Support information may be processed by ordinary email and hosting providers as necessary to deliver those services or comply with law.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational safeguards appropriate to the current limited service. No method of storage or transmission can be guaranteed completely secure.

8. Retention and deletion

Current synthetic app data is local to the test device and may be removed by clearing app data or uninstalling the app. Support correspondence is retained only as reasonably necessary to respond, resolve issues, meet legal obligations or protect the service. See the Account Deletion page for the available request route.

9. Children

TOLZA is currently intended for users aged 18 and over. It is not designed for children and does not knowingly solicit children’s personal data through this website.

10. Future production features

Production backend accounts, real OTP, saved addresses, serviceability, GPS assistance, provider integrations or analytics are not part of the current submitted build. This policy and the Google Play Data safety declaration will be updated before any materially different data practice goes live.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the product changes. The current effective date will be shown at the top of this page.

12. Contact

Privacy questions can be sent to shahvishal007@gmail.com.
