How TOLZA works

A transparent view of the current closed-test journey and the boundaries of the Alpha build.

Current build: the journey below uses synthetic demonstration products and totals. It does not connect to live retailers.

The current journey

Choose a language

The app supports English, Gujarati, Hindi, Marathi, Tamil, Telugu and Malayalam in the current foundation. Availability can change in later releases.

Use synthetic closed-test access

Approved testers follow the reviewer/tester instructions supplied with the closed test. No public account registration is offered through this website.

Search for a product

Enter a grocery query and review synthetic results intended to validate multilingual search and navigation.

Build and manage a basket

Add products, change quantities, remove items and review the basket before comparison.

Compare demonstration choices

The app compares synthetic platform totals and explains a basket recommendation. These are not live prices, stock levels or offers.

Review settings and privacy controls

Profile screens include language, saved-address foundations, location choices, support, privacy and account-deletion entry points.

What TOLZA does not currently do

  • It does not sell products or process orders.
  • It does not provide checkout, payment or delivery.
  • It does not guarantee the cheapest price.
  • It does not show live retailer inventory, stock or price.
  • It does not scrape retailer websites.
  • It does not currently provide a public web shopping service.

Production direction

Before a broad public launch, TOLZA plans to add production account services, backend-owned pincode serviceability, optional location assistance, founder/admin controls and legitimate provider integrations. Store text and policies will be updated before those capabilities are released.
