Account & Data Deletion

You can request deletion of account-linked data associated with Shopping Kart / Smart Kart. After we verify your request, the following categories may be removed:

  • Profile
  • Consent preferences
  • Saved addresses
  • Recent searches
  • Sessions
  • Linked customer records

Request deletion by email

To request account and data deletion, email shahvishal007@gmail.com with your account identifier and confirmation that you want your account-linked data deleted.

Email deletion request

This closed-test build uses email-based deletion requests. Include your account identifier so we can locate and remove your data.

Local data on your device

Data stored only on your device (such as cached searches or pincode entry) can be cleared by uninstalling the App or using your device settings to clear app storage.
