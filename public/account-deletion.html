Account & Data Deletion

Draft — not published. This page mirrors the Module 101 public deletion path (/public/account-deletion). Forms are disabled until HTTPS hosting and backend routes are provisioned.

You can request deletion of account-linked data associated with Shopping Kart / Smart Kart. After verification, the following categories may be removed:

  • Profile
  • Consent preferences
  • Saved addresses
  • Recent searches
  • Sessions
  • Linked customer records

Synthetic verification only in test environments. No real OTP or SMS is sent. Production deletion routes remain disabled by default outside approved test/development posture.
