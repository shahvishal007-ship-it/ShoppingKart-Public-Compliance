Account deletion

Use the route below to remove current test data or request deletion of account-related information.

Current Alpha: the submitted closed-test build does not create or store a production account on a TOLZA server.

Delete current closed-test app data

Use the in-app privacy route

Open Profile → Data and privacy and follow the available account/data controls shown in your version.

Remove local app data

You may also use Android Settings → Apps → TOLZA → Storage → Clear data, or uninstall the app. This removes local closed-test data from that device.

Request assistance

Email the address below if you want confirmation or need help with support correspondence associated with your request.

Web-based deletion request

Send an email to shahvishal007@gmail.com with the subject TOLZA Account Deletion Request.

Include only the minimum information needed to identify your support interaction. Do not send an OTP, password, payment information or government identification unless specifically and lawfully requested through a verified process.

What will be deleted

  • Account-related personal data controlled by TOLZA, if any exists for the relevant released service.
  • Saved addresses and preferences stored by a future production account, when such functionality is introduced.
  • Support information that is no longer required to process or document the request.

Limited retention

We may retain narrowly limited information when required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, dispute resolution or proof that a deletion request was fulfilled. Retained information will not be used for unrelated marketing.

Response process

We will acknowledge a valid request and may ask for reasonable verification. Completion time depends on the data involved and applicable obligations. Because the current Alpha does not have a production account backend, many requests will be satisfied by deleting local app data and any related support correspondence.
